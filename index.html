About Us

Since 1952

Founded in a small Parisian workshop, Diamond Jewellery began as a passion project of master craftsman Henri Laurent. What started as bespoke creations for European aristocracy has grown into an internationally renowned house of fine jewellery, while maintaining our commitment to handcrafted excellence.

Each piece in our collection carries forward Henri's original vision: jewellery should be as unique as the person who wears it.
